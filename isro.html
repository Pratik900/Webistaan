The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is the national space agency of India, established in 1969
with its headquarters in Bengaluru, Karnataka. ISRO's primary objective is to develop and harness space
technology for various applications, including satellite communication, earth observation, navigation,
scientific research, and exploration of outer space.
